Transfer vacancies you can fill

A student can report their seat as a transfer vacancy. Picking one runs an auto-chain: the vacancy's owner is displaced into another open slot or vacancy, and so on, until every displaced student settles. Tier is not a constraint here — any live vacancy or open slot works, regardless of tier. The one limit is that a single chain can route at most 2 incoming transfers into the same team, so one team can't absorb a big chunk of a chain at once. If no path settles within that limit, nothing moves.
